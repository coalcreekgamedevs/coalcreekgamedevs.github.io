"Who are you?"

We're a group of independent game developers located in and around Youngstown, Ohio.

"What do you do?"

We do all sorts of things! We hold events, participate in game jams, share resources, and make cool stuff.

"How can I get involved?"

Check out any of the cool social links above! Definitely join us on Meetup, join our Discord server, and follow us on Twitter. That way you'll be up to date on events and what not.

"Hold on a second, who's in charge here?"

That'd be me! I'm Alex, and I'm the Head Organizer of CCGD. My Co-Organizers are Kendra Corpier (she runs the Youngstown Game Developers) and Jeff Rimko.
